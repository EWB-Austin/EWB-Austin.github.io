General Quarterly Meeting - 9/9/10

Tuesday, September 07, 2010

The Greater Austin Chapter of Engineers Without Borders (EWB) will be having its quarterly chapter meeting on Thursday, September 9 at 6:30 on the UT campus in ACES 2.402. If you are a member of EWB, or are interested in finding out more about the organization, please join us.

Map of ACES 2.402: http://www.utexas.edu/maps/main/buildings/ace.html

The main agenda item will be a presentation on our Water Supply and Distribution Project in Panama. Just a few of the other items on the agenda are:

  • General overview of the organization
  • Current Board, Officer, and Project lead introductions
  • Announcement of our schedule for general meetings, executive meetings, project meetings, and meet and greets
  • ntroduction to the ongoing Mexico Computer project

We are looking forward to a very exciting year. We hope you can be a part!

Save the date! EWB-USA Regional Training Workshops will be held this fall throughout the country! Executive Committee Meetings

Saturday, April 03, 2010

Executive Committee Meetings are a general chapter meeting/workshop held the first and third Wednesday of each month at 6:30 PM at the Spider House Cafe. These meetings are forums for generating innovative ideas to move the chapter forward and create a strong foundation of support for our projects. Everyone is welcome. These meetings are a great way for folks to learn more about the organization and become involved with the chapter. Our first meeting will be April 7th.

We hope to see you there!
