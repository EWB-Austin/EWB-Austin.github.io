Soup and Bread Night

Wednesday, February 19, 2014

EWB will be participating in Soup and Bread Night, Wednesday, February 26, hosted by the Northwestern University Austin Alumni Club, at The Liberty, at 1618 1/2 E 6th.  Along with several other nonprofits, we'll be serving soup (Sopa Criolla), and bread will be provided by Easy Tiger.  There's a $10 cover to try all the soups, and all of the proceeds go to the participants.  So come out and support EWB Greater Austin, and a bunch of other worthy causes.  See you there!

Texas Ramp Project

Sunday, February 16, 2014

 

 

What better way to use our engineering skills than to build wheelchair ramps for disabled Austinites? They asked for 10, but 13 of us showed up for a morning of drilling, sanding and sawing. Thanks to the Texas Ramp Project's great planning and our enthusiasm and brawn, we got the ramp up and ready to go in 4 hours, and got invited back to help them out again! Good job, team!
"Engineers Without Borders Greater Austin- Building Ramps Just Flush"

EWB Tree Planting

Wednesday, November 20, 2013

 

EWB Greater Austin teamed up with TreeFolks to plant trees at Wooten Neighborhood Park this past weekend. 75 trees were planted! Thanks to everyone who volunteered!
