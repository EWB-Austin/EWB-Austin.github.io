2011 Year-End Fund Raising Campaign

Saturday, December 03, 2011

The goal for the 2011 Year-End Fund raising campaign is $500,000.

A handful of organizations will match dollar-for-dollar each donation up to $100,000. The secure link to pledge any donation is as follows

https://ssl.charityweb.net/ewbusa/yearend.htm

We want to have the highest number of contributors from all the chapters in the US. Come! Join us in the effort to help more communities in need around the world.

A more detailed description of what our chapter does can be found in the Holiday Letter from the chapter's co-presidents.
