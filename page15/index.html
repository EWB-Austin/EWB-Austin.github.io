Executive Committee Meetings

Saturday, April 03, 2010

Executive Committee Meetings are a general chapter meeting/workshop held the first and third Wednesday of each month at 6:30 PM at the Spider House Cafe. These meetings are forums for generating innovative ideas to move the chapter forward and create a strong foundation of support for our projects. Everyone is welcome. These meetings are a great way for folks to learn more about the organization and become involved with the chapter. Our first meeting will be April 7th.

We hope to see you there!

CPR FIRST AID TRAINING SATURDAY FEBRUARY 20TH

Wednesday, February 17, 2010

UT has an American Red Cross Club and they are offering CPR training and First Aid Training all day this Saturday February 20, and at a very reasonable price.  In addition to being a good skill to have in general, this is a great opportunity to take advantage of because EWB National  is requiring that several members on every team that travels be CPR certified and they  encourage that most members have this certification.  If you are thinking about traveling for Panama please consider this. Here is the link to the schedule (http://www.utmasscpr.org/course_info).
