AmplifyATX with Farmshare and EWB!

Saturday, March 07, 2015

Farmshare Austin is a local non-profit that aims to educate the next generation of organic vegetable growers in Central Texas and provide fresh produce to low-income families. EWB - Greater Austin has been working diligently with Farmshare Austin to help design a composting latrine, and we're currently on track to begin construction September of this year! Support your local non-profits & participate in Austin's day of giving via #AmplifyATX until 6:00 pm tonight! Help Farmshare reach their fundraising goals by donating here.

Water Trotter Urban Challenge 2015

Sunday, March 01, 2015

The Water Trotter Urban Challenge is a fun and challenging urban trekking adventure over a distance of approximately three miles. Teams will navigate through the diverse urban ecosystem of Austin’s Waller Creek Corridor exploring local neighborhoods, the Capitol and the University of Texas at Austin campus. The urban challenge is designed to emphasize the importance of clean water in developing countries.

Your sponsorship of this event will help raise resources for EWB-Austin Chapter to construct vital water infrastructure programs in Peru and Guatemala. Find out more here.

Sign up: https://wtuc2015-ewbga.eventbrite.com

We are so proud of our very own Tim Ager for winning the Professional Founders Award!

Wednesday, February 25, 2015

Eweek 2015 is all about celebrating engineers. We're excited to announce the EWB-USA award winners this week, beginning with Professional Founders Award winner Tim Ager!

Tim is a model leader in the EWB-USA community. In his role as a professional mentor and EWB-USA Greater Austin Chapter president, he has built up the organization by encouraging and developing the next generation of leaders.

Tim has been hands-on in the completion of multiple EWB-USA projects, traveling eight times to work on three different programs.

Congratulations, Tim! Keep up the great work.
