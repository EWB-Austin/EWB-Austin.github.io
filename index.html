First Meetings of Fall Semester

Weekly Meetings Begin and a Friday Social

Our first weekly Peru, Instrumentation and Local Projects meeting of the semester is this coming Monday, September 21, at 6:00pm in our rooms rented from the McCord Community Center of Austin Presbyterian Seminary, see the map below. Come help us get the new semester underway!

There's also a few officer positions we're looking to fill, including Technology Chair and Membership Chair, so let us know if you're interested.

Farmshare Austin Farm Raiser Dance and Party!

Monday, July 27, 2015

On July 31st Farmshare Austin will host its second annual Farm Raiser at the historic Saengerrunde Hall in downtown Austin. The event will feature square dancing (with lessons first!) and live music from the Barn Owls, BBQ from Tony Grasso and Moontower Coop, local vegan and vegetarian delights, and craft beverages. Proceeds will go towards Farmshare's projects locally including our Composting Toilet project.

We invite our current and prospective members to join us at this fun event while we support Farmshare and their efforts in the Austin community. Please visit their website to learn more about the event. Please contact Abdul at operations@ewbgreateraustin.org if you're interested in attending the event, EWB seats will be available on a first come first serve basis.
