EWB South Central Regional Conference

Monday, October 22, 2012

This past weekend (October 19-21st) seven EWB Greater Austin members attended the EWB South Central Regional Conference!

The conference took place in Baton Rouge, Louisiana and was hosted by the EWB-USA Louisiana State University Student Chapter.  It was a great opportunity to share experiences and learn more about how to manage cultural and social aspects of projects.

EWB Greater Austin Chapter members at the Regional Conference Banquet.  From left to right: Raquel Flinker (Co-President), Chris Lombardo (South Central Region Co-President), Corrie Thompson (Texas State Representative & Treasurer), Haley Born (Panama Project Lead), Laura Sampson (Panama Project), Alison Wood (Peru Project), Monica Edell (Peru Project)

Volunteer Opportunity at Water to Thrive 5K

Friday, September 21, 2012

Next Saturday, September 29, 2012, the Greater Austin Chapter of Engineers Without Borders-USA will be volunteering at the Water to Thrive 5K ! We will be supervising the Walk for Water which is a 25 yard path that volunteers will walk down back carrying a jerry can full of water. We will have a unique opportunity to talk to people about what it would be like to carry water like that for miles each day and some projects that help improve this situation. This will be our chance to talk about our Chapter's work and how our projects (spring boxes, distribution lines, sand filters, wells, etc.) can improve this situation. So come out and help us promote EWB-USA and raise money for clean water in Africa!

If you are interested in volunteering with EWB-USA Greater Austin, contact Haley Born at panama@ewbgreateraustin.org.
