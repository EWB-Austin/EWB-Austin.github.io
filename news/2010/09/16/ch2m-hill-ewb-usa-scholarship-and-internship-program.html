CH2M HILL EWB-USA Scholarship and Internship Program

Thursday, September 16, 2010

Our Co-Director of Finance Dhumal Aturaliye is an employee of CH2M HILL and has offered his assistance to students interested in applying. Please contact him with further questions (finance@ewbgreateraustin.org)

I am proud to announce that CH2M HILL has launched the inaugural CH2M HILL EWB-USA Scholarship and Internship Program. Highlights of the program include:

  • 10 scholarships awarded to EWB-USA student members
  • $5,000 scholarship for academic or project support (awarded fall 2010)
  • Opportunity for a paid 10-week summer 2011 internship
  • Deadline for applications is October 29 with recipients notified in early December
  • Visit www.ch2mhill.jobs/ewb to learn more and access the application

Eligibility

1)       Enrolled in a four-year accredited engineering program and continuing in the upcoming academic semester.

2)       Active, registered member of Engineers Without Borders-USA through your university chapter.

3)       Minimum GPA of 3.0.
