Spring General Meeting

Tuesday, February 03, 2015

Are you interested in international development? Do you want to use your education to create sustainable change at home and around the world?

Come join EWB for our spring general meeting! Learn about all the exciting projects EWB has ongoing and find out ways you can get involved.

Learn about our:
Peru Project
Guatemala Project
Local Latrine Project
Instrumentation Group
Water Trotter Fundraiser

All majors (engineering and non-engineering) are welcome to attend the meeting and join EWB!

The general meeting will be held in the RLM building on the UT campus (directions). Light refreshments will be served.
