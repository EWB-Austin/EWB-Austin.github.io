Three Great Events Coming Up

Monday, October 12, 2015

Thursday Happy Hour

Join us 7:30 at Scholtz Garten for beer, photo, and a chance to make impact in the lives of thousands of people! Our periodic happy hour is a great way to see of Engineers Without Borders and our fantastic projects appeal to you! We need Mechanical, Civil, Environmental, and Electrical Engineers! And we also need Computer Scientists and Technical Writers and Entrepreneurs!

Shoal Creek Clean Up

Join us Saturdy 10:00 am as we clean up Shoal Creek! We've adopted a Section of Shoal Creek in Pease Park starting at approximately 29th street. Get some fresh air and some exercise while keep Pease Park and Shoal Creek beautiful for all Austinites! Contact operations@ewbgreateraustin.org to get details and reserve your pointy stick.

Maker Faire Rome

Finally, for those of you happen to be in Rome or have a private jet, catch Mr. Anjan Contractor's talk Makers for Engineers Without Borders at Maker Faire Rome!
