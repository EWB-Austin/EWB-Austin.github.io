Save The Date - Sept. 14th - First General Meeting

EWB Greater Austin Chapter 1st Fall General Meeting

Date: Mon-Sep 14, 2015
Time: 6:30PM
Location: The University of Texas at Austin - Building CPE, Room 2.210

Engineers Without Borders Austin chapter will be holding their first meeting on the UT campus. Students and Professional from all professions are invited to join us and learn about our current and future community development projects. In addition we are excited to announce our guest speaker. More information about the meeting will be posted on our website soon.
