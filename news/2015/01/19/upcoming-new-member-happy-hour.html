Upcoming New Member Happy Hour!

Monday, January 19, 2015

Come join your favorite people (EWBer's of course) at Scholz Garten for our 1st get-together of the year and a new member happy hour! Prepare yourself for plenty of laughs, new friends and old, as we enjoy some eats & drinks beginning at 6:00 pm this Thursday (01/22).

Please invite your friends, co-workers, family and everyone else you know who might like to come! It's a beer garden and restaurant, so you don't have to be 21+ to join. We will be carpooling from UT and if you would like a ride, send an email to operations@ewbgreateraustin.org!
