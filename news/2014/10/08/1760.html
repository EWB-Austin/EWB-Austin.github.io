Chacayá Golf Tournament Fundraiser

Wednesday, October 08, 2014

Gather your friends, family, and co-workers, and join us for a fun day of golf to support The Engineers Without Borders - Greater Austin Chapter - Chacayá Water Project!

Date & Location: November 15, 2014 at the TeraVista Golf Club in Round Rock, Texas

Registration ($110/person) includes: unlimited range balls, green fees, golf cart, dinner, and a "revenge" coupon to come back and play the course again!

Schedule:
11:30 am: Registration Opens
12:30 pm: Shotgun Tournament Start
5:30 pm: Fajita buffet dinner (included), awards presentation, and silent auction

The format is a four-person  scramble, and awards will be given for the low gross team, longest drive, closest to the pin, and more. Individuals and teams are welcome!

All proceeds from this fundraising tournament will benefit the EWB-USA Greater Austin Chapter Chacayá Water Project.

To register: https://chacaya-golf.eventbrite.com

Signing up as a team? Make sure to email team member names to Rajiv Patel (rajiv33@yahoo.com)

Not a golfer, but would still like to support our efforts? Please visit below to support:
https://ssl.charityweb.net/ewbusa/pfp/ewbgacchacaywatersupplyp.htm
