Chapter receives regional award at EWB-USA national conference!

Tuesday, December 02, 2014

The Greater Austin Chapter has been selected as a 2014/2015 EWB-USA Regional Premier Chapter. This award recognizes outstanding EWB-USA Student and Professional Chapters who strive to meet the ideals of a successful EWB-USA chapter. These ideals include excellence in organization, fundraising and public relations, mentor support, and chapter and regional participation.

Premier chapters are often highlighted in EWB-USA marketing materials including fundraising campaigns, national brochures and videos, suggested as first points of contact for media interviews, story selection and more.

This award recognizes all of our dedicated members for their hard work. Thank you, and congratulations!
