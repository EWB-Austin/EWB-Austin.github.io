Soup and Bread Night

Wednesday, February 19, 2014

EWB will be participating in Soup and Bread Night, Wednesday, February 26, hosted by the Northwestern University Austin Alumni Club, at The Liberty, at 1618 1/2 E 6th.  Along with several other nonprofits, we'll be serving soup (Sopa Criolla), and bread will be provided by Easy Tiger.  There's a $10 cover to try all the soups, and all of the proceeds go to the participants.  So come out and support EWB Greater Austin, and a bunch of other worthy causes.  See you there!
