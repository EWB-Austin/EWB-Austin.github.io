Thursday November 3 : Community x Design: (re)Building Community Event

Wednesday, November 02, 2011

Come join us for Community by Design: (re) Building Community, a dynamic collaborative event showcasing locally based non-profit design efforts.  The event is free and open to the public. Donations at the door to benefit the Central Texas  Wildfire Relief efforts. There will be a raffle for door prizes.

Presenters include:
Women.Design.Build
EcoRise Youth Innovations
Partnership for a Sustainable World
Architects Without Borders - Austin
Latinos in Architecture
Design Voice
Engineers Without Borders - Austin
Austin Community Design and Development Center
Rainforest Partnership

The event is shared on our EWB Greater Austing FB page. You can access the FB event here. (http://www.facebook.com/event.php?eid=231308973590551).  Please share the event with others.

Here are the Details:

Event Title: Community x Design: (re)Building Community
Location: Treehouse  4477 S. Lamar Boulevard Austin, TX 78745
Date: November 3rd, 2011Time:7pm – 9pm (doors open 6pm)
Free and open to the public
