Sieykin, Panama Project Lead Honored

Sunday, March 13, 2011

Corrie Thompson a Co-Lead of the Sieykin, Panama-Clean Water and Health Education Project was honored by CH2M Hill with a scholarship for her outstanding dedication to academic excellence, ability to apply technical skills to make a meaningful difference, strong leadership abilities, and dedication to Engineers Without Borders-USA.

Congratulations to Corrie!
