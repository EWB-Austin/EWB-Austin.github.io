EWB-Greater Austin Chapter General Meeting

Tuesday, October 23, 2012

We have a General Meeting coming up this Thursday, 10/25/12. We will be having a wonderful guest speaker, Dr. Atila Novoselac.

Dr. Novoselac is an associate professor in the CAEE department and will be presenting "Microbes in Homes Across Cultures."

The fun starts at 7 PM in ACES 2.402. Don't forget, ALL MAJORS WELCOME!

And of course there will be cookies!
