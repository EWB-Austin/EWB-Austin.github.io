Vote for EWB-USA! Chase Community Giving Grant

Tuesday, September 18, 2012

We need YOU to vote for Engineers Without Borders USA in the Chase Community Giving program. The winners of the Chase Community Giving awards will be decided in less than 48 hours. If we win, EWB-USA will maximize our award by using it to match donations raised during the EWB-USA Year-End Giving Campaign.
EWB-USA currently has 1,370 votes. If each EWB-USA member and one friend vote today, we are guaranteed $100,000. With YOUR help, we can reach this goal. Voting ends at midnight on Wednesday, September 19.  Vote NOW!
